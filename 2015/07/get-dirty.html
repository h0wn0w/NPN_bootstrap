Get DiRT(y)

Written By: W. Howard Buddin Jr., Ph.D.
Published On: 07/08/2015

Are you in the market for a new set of research tools to help you with your next great study, article, project, paper…whatever? Have you finally had enough of old, unsupported, bloated software packages? If so, then DiRT might just be the place to start. From the web site:

“The DiRT Directory is a registry of digital research tools for scholarly use.”

That basically says it all. As a side note, one of the listings I came across – the Open Science Framework – is ridiculously cool.
