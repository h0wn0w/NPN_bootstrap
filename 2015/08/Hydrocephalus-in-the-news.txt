Title:	Hydrocephalus in the Mainstream Media
Author:	S. Marc Testa, Ph.D.
Date:	08/10/2015 
Tags:	Imaging, News, Neuropathology
Content:	Article

The [mainstream media][2] has been reporting the case of a man who, "[lost most of his brain and didn't even notice!][1]" The case was reported recently as a Lancet [Clinical Picture](http://www.thelancet.com/journals/lancet/article/PIIS0140-6736%2807%2961127-1/fulltext). At age 6 months, the man received a ventriculoatrial shunt, which was revised at age 14. His history thereafter was generally unremarkable until age 44, when he experienced only mild left leg weakness. Read more [here](http://www.thelancet.com/journals/lancet/article/PIIS0140-6736%2807%2961127-1/fulltext).
