The mainstream media has been reporting the case of a man who, "lost most of his brain and didn't even notice"! The case is reported recently as a Lancet [Clinical Picture](http://www.thelancet.com/journals/lancet/article/PIIS0140-6736%2807%2961127-1/fulltext). At age 6 months, the man received a ventriculoatrial shunt, which was revised at age 14. His histroy thereafter was generally unremarkable until age 44, when he experienced only mild left leg weakness.
