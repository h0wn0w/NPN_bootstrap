Hydrocephalus in the Mainstream Media

Written By: S. Marc Testa, Ph.D.
Published On: 08/10/2015

The mainstream media has been reporting the case of a man who, “lost most of his brain and didn’t even notice!” The case was reported recently as a Lancet Clinical Picture. At age 6 months, the man received a ventriculoatrial shunt, which was revised at age 14. His history thereafter was generally unremarkable until age 44, when he experienced only mild left leg weakness. Read more here.
