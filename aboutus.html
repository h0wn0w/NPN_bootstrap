About Neuropsych Now

Neuropsych Now is the resource for continually updated and accurate information about topics in the field of neuropsychology, and is built for students and clinicians alike.

Books

Everything old is new again.
And again. And again. That's because we've crafted downloadable books that cover the fundamentals of neuropsychology in a comprehensible, searchable format that can be read on any mobile device. Not only that, but the books are updated at least once per year. This means once you subscribe to a Neuropsych Now title, you get all the new content for a year.
All for free.

Resources

Calculators. Converters. Apps. Organizations. Blog. Information. Browse or search for quick snippets of information. Read articles that are easy to digest and offer uncommon insight. Download a converter to make scoring easier. It's all delivered right to your device, anywhere you are.
It's all right here.
Like nowhere else.
Like never before.

What is the value of Neuropsych Now?

Not convinced that easy access to the most current information is important? Consider this quote from Alexander & Roberts (1998):

“The field of clinical psychology is diverse and informational material continually becomes available in a multitude of places. Because the half-life of psychological knowledge is brief, the need for updating and accessing resources is nearly constant for both researchers and practitioners alike.”[1]
