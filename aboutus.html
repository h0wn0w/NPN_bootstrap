About Neuropsych Now

Neuropsych Now is the resource for continually updated and accurate information about topics in the field of neuropsychology, and is built for students and clinicians alike.

Books

Everything old is new again.
And again. And again. That's because we've crafted downloadable books that cover the fundamentals of neuropsychology in a comprehensible, searchable format that can be read on any mobile device. Want more? We offer an a la carte subscription that allows you to download only the titles that you want, and nothing you don't. And it gets better. Your subscription to any Neuropsych Now title allows you to receive any changes or updates that we make to the publication for the term of your subscription.

What is the value of Neuropsych Now?

Not convinced that easy access to the most current information is important? Consider this quote from Alexander & Roberts (1998):

“The field of clinical psychology is diverse and informational material continually becomes available in a multitude of places. Because the half-life of psychological knowledge is brief, the need for updating and accessing resources is nearly constant for both researchers and practitioners alike.”[1]
