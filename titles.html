Neuropsych Now Titles

Our mission is to provide accurate and reliable information on topics germane to the study and practice of neuropsychology. Each title is broken down into two sections:

  1. A brief review of the fundamentals and history of the topic.
  2. Discussion of the most current literature on the topic
This scheme provides benefits of both a text book (fundamentals) and primary sources (concensus).
Titles will be available for purchase from the iTunes store, Amazon and direct download from Neuropsych Now.
