Guides & Books

Our primary goal is the production of focused, easily accessible titles about various topics central to the study and practice of neuropsychology. We call these titles "State of the Field Guides", because they are written, from beginning to end, using only information from the most recent, highest quality, and keystone studies that define the state of the art in neuropsychology. Each title begins with a discussion of sound fundamentals, guiding you through core points of the topic. Following this is a review of systematically chosen articles that define the current concensus of the field.

We work to continuously produce concise, affordable, and easily accessible titles, designed for both the neuropsychology clinician and student alike. Occasionally, we'll release a longer title of greater depth and breadth. Each work is carefully curated and written so that it contains only the most reliable, accurate and current information.

Titles will be available for purchase from the iTunes store and Amazon beginning in Autumn 2013. Please if you would like to receive advance notice of our inaugural release. Until then, make sure to check out the rest of the site for helpful tools and informative articles!
