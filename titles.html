Guides & Books: Coming Soon!

Our primary goal is to produce focused, easily accessible information on various topics central to the study and practice of neuropsychology. We call these titles "State of the Field", because they are written, from beginning to end, using only information from the most recent, highest quality keystone studies that define the state of the art in neuropsychology. Each begins with a discussion of fundamentals, guiding you through core points of the topic. Following this is a review of systematically chosen articles that define the current concensus of the field.

The majority of our productions will be approximately 5,000 words in length. We call them "Guides," as they are designed to guide you through a very focused topic. Occasionally, we'll release a work of greater depth and breadth. These will be on the order of 8,000 words or more.

Titles will be available for purchase from the iTunes store and Amazon beginning in Autumn 2013. Please if you would like to receive advance notice of our inaugural release. Until then, make sure to check out the rest of the site for helpful tools and Informative articles!
