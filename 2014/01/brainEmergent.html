The Human Connectome Project

The Human Connectome Project is a relatively new endeavor designed to map the brain’s neural connections. The researchers clearly put in a good bit of effort on one of their recent studies, and it’s a good read. There have been some nice advances over the past year or so. It will be exciting to watch these projects evolve along with our understanding of functional anatomy.
