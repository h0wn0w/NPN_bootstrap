OmniFocus and GTD Resources

Written By: S. Marc Testa, Ph.D.
Published On: 01/10/2016

It’s no secret, we think GTD can be used as an effective executive functioning intervention. OmniFocus or Doit.im are, respectively, apps for iPhone and Android that can be used to implement GTD. For those of you who can’t get enough of GTD or for peeps who want a good set of resources, we recommend the following:

  • In 2013, The OmniGroup held a symposium of sorts and had several internet types talk about how they use OmniFocus and GTD. The collection of videos can be found here.
  • David Sparks is a business attorney, and a geek, and a podcaster, and a blogger, and an author. His website MacSparky is an excellent productivity resource. He has created an OmniFocus Video Field Guide that can be found up in here.
