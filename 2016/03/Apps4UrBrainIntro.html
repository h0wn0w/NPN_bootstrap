Apps4UrBrain: Intro

Written By: S. Marc Testa, Ph.D.
Published On: 03/24/2016

In line with our focus on providing the general public with useful neuropsychology information, we are introducing #Apps4UrBrain. We will review apps that can be used to enhance everyday cognition functioning. These will be brief reviews of each app’s best features. A list of strengths and weaknesses will also be included.
