About the Guides and Books

Our primary goal is the production of easily accessible information on focused topics that are central to the study and practice of neuropsychology.

Each title begins with a discussion of fundamentals, guiding you through core points of the topic. Following this is a review of systematically chosen articles that define the current concensus of the field. We call these titles “State of the Field”, because they are written, from beginning to end, using only information from the most recent, highest quality studies that define the state of the art in neuropsychology.

The majority of our productions will be approximately 5,000 words in length. We call them "Guides," as they are designed to guide you through a very focused topic. Occasionally, we'll release a work of greater depth and breadth. These will be on the order of 8,000 words or more. Because we author only focused topics, you can create an a la carte experience by purchasing and downloading only the titles that you want and need, and none that you don't.

Titles will be available for purchase from the iTunes store and Amazon beginning in Autumn 2013. Please if you would like to receive advance notice of our inaugural release. Until then, make sure to check out the rest of the site for helpful tools and Informative articles!
