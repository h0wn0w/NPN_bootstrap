Neuropsych Now Blog

The Neuropsych Now blog is our chance to deliver content on a variety of topics, some of which you're unlikely to find in any other neuropsychology news source. Of course, we'll talk about the clinical topics that we all know and love.
This blog is not a forum for only our opinions, and we absolutely want others to help make this blog a varied source of information! If you're interested in writing a post, plese contact Dr. Buddin or Dr. Testa with an idea or topic, and one of us will get back to you as soon as we're able.
