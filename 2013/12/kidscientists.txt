Title:				Kids Are Scientists, Too
Author:				W. Howard Buddin Jr., Ph.D.
Category:           Article
Date:                   12/12/2013 
Tags:                   News, Research

Children - we probably underestimate their potential for accomplishment too often. Well, now it looks as though they've caught us flat-footed, and decided to [head up their own peer-reviewed journal](http://blogs.smithsonianmag.com/smartnews/2013/11/re-publishing-a-peer-reviewed-journal/). This is incredible, and is exactly the kind of exposure to science that so few people ever get, at any age.
