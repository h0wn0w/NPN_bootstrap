Kids Are Scientists, Too

Written By: W. Howard Buddin Jr., Ph.D.
Published On: 12/12/2013

Children - we probably underestimate their potential for accomplishment too often. Well, now it looks as though they’ve caught us flat-footed, and decided to head up their own peer-reviewed journal. This is incredible, and is exactly the kind of exposure to science that so few people ever get, at any age.
